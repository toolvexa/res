FAQ

Your frequently asked questions.

Is Kickresume really free? +
Yes! You can create and download a basic resume for free. Premium plans unlock additional templates, AI features, and unlimited downloads. No credit card required to get started.
Are Kickresume templates ATS-friendly? +
All our templates are tested for Applicant Tracking System compatibility. We ensure proper formatting, standard fonts, and clean layouts that pass ATS filters at top companies.
How does the AI resume writer work? +
Simply describe your experience in plain language and our AI will transform it into professional, impactful bullet points. You can regenerate, edit, or accept any suggestion. The AI is trained on thousands of successful resumes from top companies.
Can I import my LinkedIn profile? +
Yes! You can import your LinkedIn profile data directly into Kickresume to quickly pre-fill your resume, then customize it with our templates and AI tools.
What file formats can I export my resume in? +
You can download your resume as a PDF (recommended for most job applications) or share it via a unique link. PDF exports are print-ready and pixel-perfect across all devices.
